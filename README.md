⚡ Core Features
🔫 Advanced Aimbot
Built for absolute precision or mimicking legit gameplay—your choice.

Dynamic FOV Targeting: Define your exact activation area with a customizable visual FOV circle.
Humanized Smoothing: Separate X and Y smoothing controls to make your mechanical aim look completely natural.
Precision Bone Selection: Granular targeting—choose between Head, Neck, Chest, Stomach, or Pelvis.
Smart Multi-Point Scanning: Automatically scans adjacent bones to guarantee the best possible target acquisition.
Per-Weapon Overrides: Granular control over your settings depending on if you are holding a Pistol, Rifle, or Sniper.
Sanity Checks: Built-in Flash & Visibility checking (checks for walls and smokes) so you never lock onto something you shouldn't.
🎯 Intelligent Triggerbot
Never miss a crucial shot again.

Reaction Delay: Millisecond-accurate custom delays before firing to simulate human reaction times.
Burst Fire Mode: Define your burst count and delay for flawless semi-auto or burst-fire weapon control.
Awareness Checks: Automatically disables when flashed or when the target is obscured by walls/smokes.
🕹️ Standalone RCS (Recoil Control System)
Totally independent recoil control with highly adjustable X/Y continuous compensation scales. Keep those sprays incredibly tight without utilizing the aimbot.
👁️ Visuals & ESP
👤 Player ESP
Deep situational awareness with customizable aesthetics.

Box & Skeleton: Standard or Corner boxes with adjustable thickness, alongside highly detailed skeleton bone rendering.
Dynamic Head Circle: A perfectly scalable circle hugging the enemy's head.
Rich Information Tags: Track Name, Current Weapon, Distance, Health (Dynamic Bar), Armor (Bar + Text), and Ammo (Bar).
Off-Screen Indicators: Intuitive directional arrows for threats residing outside of your current FOV.
Visibility Coloring: Elements dynamically shift colors the moment an enemy breaks cover and becomes visible.
🌍 World ESP
Loot Tracing: Accurately renders dropped ground weapons, showing both their name and dynamically scaled distance.
🎨 Deep Customization
Interactive Layout Editor: Drag, drop, and snap ESP elements (Names, Health, Weapon, etc.) to the Top, Bottom, Left, or Right of the player box!
Anti-Flash: Completely neuter the blinding effect of flashbangs—see clearly when they want you blind.
⚙️ Stealth, System & Performance
🥷 Covert Operations
Application Disguise: Tucks itself away seamlessly. Automatically renames its window/console to "Notepad" and hijacks the notepad.exe icon to vanish from plain sight in your taskbar.
Stream-Proof ("Bypass Capture"): completely hides the overlay from all screen recording, screenshotting, and streaming software (OBS, Discord, XSplit, etc.).
🚀 Peak Performance
Low Spec Mode: Instantly strip away heavy visual effects to maintain peak FPS on lower-end rigs.
Custom ESP Rate: Dial in your exact ESP refresh rate (in milliseconds) for the perfect balance of smoothness and performance.
Priority Control: Elevate the application's process priority directly from the menu.
🛠️ Utility & Misc
Movement (Bhop): Automated, flawless bunny hopping with a customizable jump delay for buttery smooth movement.
Engine Radar Hack: Forces all enemies to appear directly on your in-game CS2 radar.
Spectator Warning: A sleek floating window alerting you the exact moment someone is spectating you or your target.
Live Keybinds Window: A beautifully rendered UI panel tracking all your active feature toggles and their real-time states.
Force Crosshair: Overlay a custom crosshair (great for snipers and noscopes!) with total control over size, thickness, and center dot.
Hit Feedback System:
Hit Sounds: Play custom .wav files upon landing a hit or securing a kill.
Hitmarkers: Screen-centered, animated impact markers with completely adjustable duration and fade out.
🔮 Theming & Configs
Full Theme Engine: Switch between gorgeous built-in palettes like Midnight Blue and Oceanic.
Live Theme Editor: Take total control over the UI. Build your own perfect aesthetic with comprehensive color customization.
Profile System: Save, load, and hot-swap different config files for entirely different playstyles (Legit, Semi-Rage, etc.) in a matter of seconds.
Enjoy the release, study the code, and have fun. Remember to keep those offsets updated!
